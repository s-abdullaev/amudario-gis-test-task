## English

**GIS Engineer (Geospatial + GeoAI) — Amudar.io · Tashkent · On-site**

Amudar.io builds AI-powered IoT for smart agriculture — used by global organizations, banks, research institutions, and users across Uzbekistan. We're hiring a GIS engineer to build the geospatial backbone of our platform: turning satellite tiles, drone orthomosaics, and ground-station data into maps and reports.

**What you'll do**
- Build backend pipelines that pull Sentinel-1/2, Landsat, and commercial satellite imagery on schedule; ingest drone orthomosaics and DSMs uploaded by field teams.
- Fuse imagery with on-site weather and pest-trap station data into useful agricultural and ecological products: LULC, crop monitoring (type, condition, phenology, yield), soil degradation, anomaly maps, frost / drought / pest risk layers.
- Design interactive maps and dashboards: tile-served raster layers, vector field boundaries, user annotations, time-series overlays.
- Apply **GeoAI** for segmentation, classification, and change detection across drone and satellite imagery.
- Integrate everything cleanly into the existing Amudar.io apps.

**What we expect**
- Strong with **PostGIS, GDAL/OGR, GeoPandas, rasterio / xarray, STAC, Cloud Optimized GeoTIFF (COG), Zarr, vector tiles / PMTiles**.
- Comfortable with **tile servers** (TiTiler, GeoServer, MapServer) and **frontend map libs** (Leaflet, MapLibre / Mapbox GL, OpenLayers, deck.gl).
- Familiar with **EO data platforms**: Sentinel Hub, Google Earth Engine, Microsoft Planetary Computer.
- Drone-data fluent: OpenDroneMap / WebODM or Pix4D / Metashape outputs, orthorectification, georeferencing.
- **GeoAI**: hands-on with SAM / samgeo, torchgeo, raster-vision, geospatial foundation models, and modern segmentation / change-detection models for drone and satellite imagery.
- **Python** first (FastAPI, GeoPandas / rasterio); comfortable in **TypeScript / Angular** when shipping the map UI; familiar with **Prefect / Airflow**-style orchestration.
- AI-native: you ship daily with Claude Code, Cursor, or equivalent agentic tooling — and you know where to *not* trust it.
- High ownership: see a problem, design the fix, ship it, integrate it with the existing platform.

**Offer**
Negotiable salary · On-site in Tashkent, 5 days/week, flexible hours · Real work experience building a scalable IoT + Earth-Observation ecosystem.
